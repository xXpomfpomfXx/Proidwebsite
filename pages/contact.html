Frequently Asked Questions

How does Mindcraft help with DMP2025?

We make DMP2025 relevant to youths through gamified learning and ensure your ideas reach Singapore's urban planning authorities.

Do I need urban planning knowledge?

No! We're designed for youths who want to learn. We make urban planning fun and relevant to your daily life.

How do I share my ideas for Singapore's future?

Complete our quest stations, participate in Minecraft challenges, or contact us directly to share your vision.
